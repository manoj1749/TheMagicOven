From Starter to Hearth: Crafting the Perfect Sourdough

A Journey in Three Acts - Nurturing, Fermenting, and Baking Excellence


Cultivating the Starter

It starts with a mixture of flour and water that captures wild yeast and bacteria from the environment to develop a robust microbial community.


Fermenting the Dough

Then we Integrate a portion of the sourdough starter into the mix and knead the dough thoroughly. Allowing the dough to undergo a series of fermentation stages, during which it develops flavor and texture.


Shaping and Baking

Once the dough has fermented to perfection, carefully shape it into the desired form we Bake it until the crust achieves a golden-brown hue and the internal temperature is ideal.
