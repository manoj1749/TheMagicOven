Detailed Description

Our Plain Sourdough Bread is a testament to the timeless art of artisan baking, blending the finest bread flour and whole wheat flour to create a harmonious and wholesome loaf. Carefully crafted by our skilled bakers, this bread is a celebration of simplicity and natural goodness.

The foundation of this bread lies in the high-quality bread flour, known for its protein content that imparts structure and texture to the loaf. Complemented by the addition of whole wheat flour, our Plain Sourdough offers a nuanced flavor profile with a subtle nuttiness and earthiness.

The fermentation process, a hallmark of sourdough baking, is given the time it deserves to develop a robust and tangy flavor profile. This slow fermentation not only enhances the taste but also contributes to the distinctive texture – a crisp and golden-brown crust that gives way to an open and airy crumb.

As you slice through the loaf, you'll discover the delightful marriage of a chewy interior and a crust that offers a satisfying crunch. The whole wheat flour introduces a depth of flavor, providing a nutritional boost with added fiber and nutrients.

Perfect for sandwiches, toasting, or simply enjoyed on its own, our Plain Sourdough Bread is a versatile choice for those who appreciate the purity of traditional bread-making. Each bite reflects the dedication we have to delivering a premium, handcrafted loaf that captures the essence of authentic sourdough.
